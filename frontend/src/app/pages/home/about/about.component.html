ABOUT

Skateire is your go-to online directory for skateparks and pump tracks across Ireland. Whether you're into skateboarding, scooters, BMX, or inline skating, we've got all the details you need on the best (and worst) parks around the country. This site is built by skateboarders, with the aim of being a valuable resource for the Irish skate community. If you'd like to see your local park featured here, just drop us a line via our contacts page. To get started click on Skateparks below or via the navigation bar.
